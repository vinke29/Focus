Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

What we collect

When you create an account, we store your email address and display name. When you use Kokoon, we store your focus session history, creature collection, and achievement progress.

How we use it

Your data powers your personal experience — your stats, your collection, and your streaks. We don't sell your data, we don't run ads, and we don't share your information with third parties.

Authentication

We use Supabase for authentication. You can sign up with email or Google. Passwords are hashed and never stored in plain text.

Cookies & local storage

Kokoon uses local storage to keep your session active and cache your collection for offline use. We don't use tracking cookies or analytics scripts.

Data retention

Your data is kept as long as your account exists. You can delete your account and all associated data at any time from the app settings.

Contact

Questions about your data? Reach us at hello@kokoon.app.
