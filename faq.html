FAQ

What is Kokoon?

Kokoon is a focus timer that turns your work sessions into a creature-collecting game. Complete a focus session, hatch a creature. Build your collection over time.

How do I hatch creatures?

Start a focus session (25, 45, or 60 minutes). When the timer completes, an egg hatches into a random creature. Longer sessions have better odds of hatching rare and legendary creatures.

What are variants?

Each creature can appear in four color variants: Standard, Gold, Crimson, and Void. Standard is the most common. Void is extremely rare — roughly 1 in 1,000 hatches.

What is fusion?

If you hatch a duplicate creature, you can fuse it with your existing one to upgrade its variant. Two Standards make a Gold, two Golds make a Crimson, and two Crimsons make a Void.

How many creatures are there?

There are 60 creatures across five regions: Japan, Americas, Europe, Africa, and Fantasy. New regions unlock as you complete more sessions.

Do I need an account?

You can use Kokoon without an account — your data saves locally. Creating an account lets you sync across devices and appear on the public profile page.

How much does Kokoon cost?

Kokoon is a one-time purchase of $4.99. No subscriptions, no ads, no in-app purchases. Pay once, keep everything forever — including all future updates.

Can I delete my account?

Yes. You can delete your account and all associated data from the app settings at any time.
